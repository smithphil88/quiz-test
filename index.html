The Rules

  • Enter a username
  • Choose a game type - either Guess the Shirt or Guess the Year.
  • Click a choice from the given suggestions.
  • Answer all questions to finish the quiz.
  • Click on home to restart and try again

Guess the Shirt

You will be shown a 'famous' football shirt from the past 40 years...or so.

All you have to do a select the correct answer from four potential teams

This is the 'easy' mode - continue if you know about the infamous 'grey' kit at the Dell.

Good luck!

Guess the Year

You will be shown another shirt from a team within the last 40 years.

Your goal is to choose the correct year that the kit was worn in the photo.

Only enter if you think you know your Gianfranco Zolas from your Michael Duberrys!

Good luck!
